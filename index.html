Chocolate Cake

Ingredients:

What You'll Need:

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp baking powder

Steps:

Let's Bake:

🟣Step 1: Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
🟢Step 2: Mix all ingredients well.
🔵Step 3: Pour into baking tray and bake the mixture for 30 minutes.
🟠Step 4: Let it cool and serve!

⏱️Preparation Time: 10 minutes

🍽️Serves: 4–6 servings
